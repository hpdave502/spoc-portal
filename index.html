SPOC: UK & Ireland

Introduction

This SPOC (Single Point of Contact) document serves as a comprehensive knowledge hub for UK and Ireland accounting operations. It covers payroll, compliance, taxation, and other crucial business obligations, with actionable templates and deadlines for reference.

United Kingdom

1. Payroll Process

  • Monthly Payroll Cycle: Employee inputs cut-off by 20th, review, and finalization by 25th.
  • Submission to HMRC (FPS) by or before payday.
  • Employer Payment Summary (EPS) if required – before the 19th of the following month.

2. Tax Payments

  • PAYE/NIC due by 22nd of the following month.
  • Corporation Tax – 9 months and 1 day after end of accounting period.

3. Statutory Filings

  • Confirmation Statement: Annually, usually due 14 days after the anniversary of incorporation.
  • Accounts Filing: Within 9 months of year-end to Companies House.

Ireland

1. Payroll Process

  • Payroll deadline: Inputs by 20th, file to Revenue before payday.
  • Submission via ROS using Payroll Software or manual file.

2. VAT Filing

  • Bi-monthly VAT return and payment due by 19th of the month following the period.
  • ROS submission via VAT3 Form.

3. Corporation Tax

  • Return and payment due 9 months after year-end (Form CT1).
